Fudgy Vegan Brownies

This one is for my chocolate lovers! If you would be happy swimming in a bowl of dark chocolate, you're going to love this recipe. This brownies are simple, rich, and so delicious!

Ingredients

  1. 1/4 cup well-mashed avocado
  2. 1/4 cup avocado oil
  3. 1 cup flour
  4. 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  5. 3/4 cup sugar
  6. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  7. 1 teaspoon salt
  8. 2/3 cup water
  9. 1 1/2 cups vegan chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit. Lightly grease an 8-inch square pan with cooking spray.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the mashed avocado and oil to combine.
  3. Add in the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking soda, vanilla extract, salt, and water, and stir until combined.
  4. Gently gold in 1 cup of the chocolate chips until combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and spread it in an even layer. Sprinkle evenly with the remaining 1/2 cup chocolate chips.

  7. Remove the pan from the oven and let cool for at least 20 minutes before serving.
  8. Cut into squares and dive in!!
