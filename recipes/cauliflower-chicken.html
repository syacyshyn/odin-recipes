General Tso's Cauliflower Chick'n Recipe

This General Tso's Cauliflower Chick'n is my go-to recipe for dinner parties. Especially if I'm serving non-vegans! They're always so amazed and left wanting more, I swear. The sauce is absolutely to die for, and once deep fried the cauliflower ends up with a tender and delicious consistency.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cauliflower florets

For batter:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup cornflour
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • water as needed

For Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons seasame oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, grated
  • 1 inch giner, grated
  • 6 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1/3 cup vegetable stock

For Garnish:

  • seasame seeds
  • green onions

Instructions

  1. Wash and completely dry the cauliflower florets.
  2. In a mixing bowl add the ingredients for the batter except the water. Slowly add the water to make a pancake batter consistency batter to coat the florets.
  3. Heat oil on medium in deep pan/skillet with enough oil to submerge the florets.
  4. Once the florets are crispy (approx. 8 mins), drain them on a napkin.
  5. In a seperate skillet, add the ingredients for sauce except for the cornstarch and water mixture.
  6. Bring to a boil while consistently stirring, add the cornstarch and water mixture, and take off the heat.
  7. Keep stirring the sauce until it thickens.
  8. Pour the suace over the fried cauliflower florets. Toss well and sprinkle seasame seeds and chopped green onion on top for serving!
  9. Enjoy!!
